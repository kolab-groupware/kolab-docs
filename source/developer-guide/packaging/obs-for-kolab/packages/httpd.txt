.. _package-httpd:

httpd
=====

The **httpd** package is supplied for Debian Wheezy, as a requirement
for the 389 Directory Server and for Kolab Enterprise 13 and Kolab
Enterprise 14 to enable additional SSL/TLS configuration options.
